# PRODUCT_IDENTITY.md

## What PRAN Is

PRAN is a **Medical Evidence Intelligence Platform**.

It is a research workstation for exploring medical evidence — guidelines, trials, conflicts, and the canvas between them.

## What PRAN Is NOT

- Medical chatbot
- Symptom checker
- Healthcare CRM
- PDF chat
- RAG wrapper
- Medical dashboard
- Hospital ERP

## Core Identity

PRAN treats **evidence** as the primary object. Everything derives from evidence. Every piece of evidence has provenance: source, citation, confidence, metadata, and relationships.

The product is built for clinicians, researchers, and medical intelligence analysts who need to synthesize vast amounts of clinical literature into actionable understanding.

## Flagship Features

1. **Medical Courtroom** — Adversarial debate engine that pitches treatments against each other using real evidence
2. **Evidence Pyramid** — Hierarchical classification of evidence quality (meta-analyses at apex, case reports at base)
3. **Canvas** — Spatial evidence board for visual exploration of research relationships
4. **Timeline** — Chronological evolution of clinical evidence
5. **Knowledge Graph** — Entity relationships between diseases, treatments, drugs, guidelines, and research

## Data Sources

Primary:

- PubMed (NCBI E-utilities)
- ClinicalTrials.gov (APIv2)
- OpenFDA (drug labels, FAERS adverse events)

Future:

- WHO
- NICE
- CDC
- AHA
- Medical guideline repositories

## Design Philosophy

- Scholarly paper aesthetic — warm archival tones, editorial serif typography
- No medical clichés (no stethoscopes, caduceus, heartbeat lines)
- Information density over decorative elements
- Monospace metadata, serif display type, sans-serif body
- Every pixel should reinforce "serious medical intelligence platform"
